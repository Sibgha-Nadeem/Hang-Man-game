WELCOME TO HANGMAN

  • In ONE-PLAYER Hangman, you guess letters to reveal a hidden word before a stick figure is fully drawn.
  • The game starts with dashes representing the word's letters.
  • You guess letters, and if correct, they appear in the word.
  • Incorrect guesses add a part to the stick figure (head, body, limbs).
  • The game ends when you guess the word or the stick figure is complete.

TIPS

  • Use your vowels. Looking at vowels is a good way to start.
  • After trying your vowels, try out a few letters like E and T to see if they appear in the word!
  • Break down phrases.
  • Don't forget your Js and Zs.
